Basic Setup

The Basic setup for Locations can be performed in Settings/Location Settings on admin dashboard.

Add Office Branches

To add an office branch:

  • Login to Admin dashboard and head over to Settings => Location Settings
  • Under "Office Branches" section, Click "Add Branch" button on the top right corner
  • Fill in the approprite detils like Zipcode, Office Address, Phone.
  • Choose the status of the Branch
  • Choose weather you want the branch to show up on homepage and contact us page.
  • Save the branch

Add Pickup / Delivery Locations

Right here, you can add the pickup and delivery locations of your company as well as the prices.

The location added here are what will be shown to users if they choose company pickup/delivery during checkout.

  • Login to Admin dashboard and head over to Settings => Location Settings
  • Under "Pickup and Delivery Locations" section, Click "Add Location" button on the top right corner
  • Add the details of the location, as well as the Pickup and Delivery charge
  • Save the location
